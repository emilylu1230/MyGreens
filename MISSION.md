# Mission: Indoor Plant Care

## Why
Emily wants to keep her 11 indoor/outdoor plants alive and thriving as a relaxing spare-time hobby. She lives in Adelaide, South Australia (Mediterranean climate — hot dry summers, mild winters) and has a master bedroom with a large openable window, curtains for light control, and easy access to an outdoor space. She already tracks her plants with nicknames and a seasonal plan, and has propagation in progress.

## Success looks like
- Knows the name, light needs, and watering schedule for each plant she owns
- Can diagnose common problems (yellowing leaves, drooping, root rot) before they become fatal
- Has a simple routine that keeps all her plants healthy through Adelaide's seasonal extremes
- Knows when and how to move plants between indoors and outdoors safely

## Constraints
- Spare time hobby — lessons should be short and practical
- One main room setup: master bedroom, large window, curtains, access to outdoor space
- Adelaide climate: hot summers (40°C+), mild winters, low humidity indoors in summer

## Out of scope
- Commercial growing or selling plants
- Propagation and advanced techniques (until basics are solid)
- Hydroponics or specialty growing systems
