# Indoor Plant Care Resources

## Knowledge

- [The Sill — Plant Care Library](https://www.thesill.com/blogs/care-miscellany)
  Practical, species-specific care guides written by horticulturalists. Use for: watering schedules, light requirements, common problems per species.

- [Greg App Plant Database](https://greg.app/plant-care/)
  Community-sourced care data with climate adjustments. Use for: watering frequency tailored to location and season.

- [University of Adelaide — Gardening in South Australia](https://www.adelaide.edu.au/library/find/databases/)
  Local context for Adelaide climate. Use for: seasonal care, heat stress management, outdoor transition timing.

- [RHS (Royal Horticultural Society) — Houseplants](https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/types/houseplants)
  High-trust reference from a long-established horticultural authority. Use for: species identification, propagation basics, pest identification.

- [Bloomscape Plant Care Guides](https://bloomscape.com/plant-care-guide/)
  Beginner-friendly care guides with clear watering and light explanations. Use for: introductory lessons, light level definitions.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/houseplants](https://www.reddit.com/r/houseplants/)
  Large, well-moderated community for general indoor plant questions. Use for: plant ID help, problem diagnosis with photos.

- [r/plantclinic](https://www.reddit.com/r/plantclinic/)
  Dedicated to diagnosing sick plants. Use for: when a plant is struggling and you need a second opinion.

- [Adelaide & SA Gardeners Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/adelaidegardeners/)
  Local gardeners with Adelaide-specific knowledge. Use for: seasonal timing, heat wave advice, local nursery recommendations.
